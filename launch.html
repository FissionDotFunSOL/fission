Launch a Derivative

Five steps: pick a launchpad, peg a stock, set your strategy, deploy, register. The engine handles everything after that.

Pick Your Launchpad

Where will your token live? Fill works with the top Robinhood Chain launchpads.

Before you start: you'll need a wallet with a little ETH on Robinhood Chain (0.0005 ETH launch fee + gas). No ETH there yet? Bridge from Ethereum with the official Arbitrum bridge (pick Robinhood Chain as the destination, ~10 min) or faster routes like Across. Chain ID 4663 — beware of fake "bridge" sites.

Pick a Stock & Direction

Choose the stock your token tracks, the direction, a leverage cap, and how aggressively the bot should trade.

ⓘ Stock perps trade only while the US market is open (Mon–Fri 9:30am–4:00pm ET, plus extended sessions per strategy). Creator fees collect around the clock and queue for the next session.

Selected: LONG @ up to 50x · DEGEN

Name Your Derivative

Choose a ticker symbol. This will be the token name on your launchpad.

Setup on Pons

Create your token with these exact settings.

Verify & Register

Paste your token address. We verify the on-chain configuration.

What happens after you register
The engine claims your creator fees automatically, trades stock perps on Ostium under the strategy you chose, and uses profits to buy back and burn your token (70%) and FILL (30%). Watch it all live on the dashboard.
