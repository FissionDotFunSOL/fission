FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FISSION?
FISSION is a protocol that turns Pump.fun creator fees into an autonomous trading and buyback engine. When you launch a token on Pump.fun and register it with FISSION, 100% of your creator fees are used to open leveraged perpetual positions on Jupiter Perps. Profits from those positions buy back and burn your token, creating deflationary pressure.
What happens if the position gets liquidated?
At high leverage (250x), positions can be liquidated by small price movements. When this happens, the collateral for that position is lost. However, the protocol continuously claims new fees and opens new positions. A fast profit-checker runs every 60-90 seconds to take profits before positions can reverse. Over time, the cumulative buyback pressure exceeds the liquidation losses.
How are fees split?
Creator fees are split 70/30. 70% goes to leveraged perpetual positions on Jupiter Perps. 30% goes directly to buying back and burning the FISSION protocol token. When positions profit, those profits are split again: 70% buys back the derivative token (the one whose fees funded the position), 30% buys back FISSION.
Is the code audited?
The code is fully open source on GitHub. Anyone can review the complete protocol logic, fee claiming, position management, and buyback execution. No formal audit has been conducted. Use at your own risk.
What is the FISSION token?
FISSION is the protocol token. 30% of all creator fees and 30% of all position profits across every token launched on the platform are used to buy back and burn FISSION. The more tokens launched and the more fees generated, the more buy pressure on FISSION.
Can I verify transactions on-chain?
Yes. Every fee claim, position open/close, buyback swap, and token burn is a Solana transaction signed by the protocol wallet. You can verify all activity on Solscan. The protocol wallet address is displayed in the footer and links directly to the Solscan explorer.
RISK WARNING
FISSION Protocol uses high-leverage perpetual positions (up to 250x) which carry extreme risk. Positions can be liquidated within minutes of opening. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The protocol is experimental and unaudited. There is no guarantee of profit or return of capital. Tokens launched on Pump.fun are highly speculative. Do not invest more than you can afford to lose. This is not financial advice.
